## ğŸ’¡ Tips for Best Recognition

### 1. Lighting
- **Bright, even lighting** from the front
- **Avoid backlighting** (don't sit in front of window)
- **No harsh shadows** on hands
- **Consistent lighting** throughout gesture

### 2. Hand Position
- **Center of frame** - Keep hands in middle of video
- **2-3 feet away** from camera
- **Chest/face level** - Don't hold hands too low or high
- **Both hands visible** if using two hands

### 3. Gesture Clarity
- **Hold for 1-2 seconds** - Don't move too fast
- **Clear finger positions** - Make distinct shapes
- **Steady hands** - Avoid shaking
- **Distinct transitions** - Pause between different signs

### 4. Background
- **Solid color** wall behind you works best
- **Minimal clutter** - Remove busy backgrounds
- **No hand-colored walls** - Avoid skin-tone backgrounds
- **Good contrast** between hands and background

### 5. Camera Setup
- **Eye level** or slightly below
- **Stable position** - Don't move camera
- **Good quality webcam** - 720p minimum
- **Clean lens** - Wipe camera lens

### 6. Practice
- **Try in front of mirror first**
- **Start with simple signs** (HELLO, YES, TWO)
- **Check confidence score** (aim for 70%+)
- **Review this guide** before each session
